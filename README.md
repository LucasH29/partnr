# Partnr

**Find the right person to build with — globally.**

Partnr matches high school students across schools and countries by skills, role, and goals. Whether you're building a startup, launching a nonprofit, doing research, or competing in a global competition — find the co-founder, research partner, or teammate who has exactly the skills you need.

## The problem

Most ambitious students have the drive to build something real. What they're missing is the right partner. Google Classroom and Discord help you work with people you already know. Paying for a mentor or joining an accelerator costs money most students don't have. No platform exists to find the *right person* — matched by skill level, role, and what you're actually trying to build — across schools, cities, and countries.

Partnr is that platform.

## What Partnr does

- **AI-powered matching** by skill level, role (CEO / CTO / Research Lead), and target project or competition
- **Role-complementary** — a CEO gets matched with a CTO, a researcher gets matched with an engineer
- **Skill search** — find anyone globally by skill, even students without a project yet
- **All project types** — startups, nonprofits, research projects, competition teams, creative collaborations
- **Only team-eligible opportunities included** — every match is actionable
- **Global** — 150+ countries, timezone and language aware
- **Safe** — school email verified, no private messages until both students accept
