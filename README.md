# Partnr

Find your perfect project partner globally.

Partnr matches high school students across schools and countries 
by skills, role, and competition. Built for students who want to 
compete in ISEF, Conrad Challenge, hackathons, DECA, and more 
but can't find the right partner.

## The problem
Google Classroom and Discord help you work with people you already 
know. No platform helps you find the right person to work with 
across schools, cities, or countries.

## What Partnr does
- AI-powered matching by skill level, role (CEO/CTO/Research Lead), 
  and target competition
- Only team-eligible competitions included, every match is actionable
- Global - 50+ countries, timezone and language aware
- Safe, school email verified, no private messages until both 
  students accept

## Tracks
Research & Science · Build a Business · Social Impact · 
Arts & Writing · Engineering & Tech

## Built with
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Claude AI (claude-sonnet-4-6)
